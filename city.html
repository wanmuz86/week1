Taman Equine

Galleria Equine Park is strategically located in Seri Kembangan. Galleria Equine Park is surrounded by various known amenities and facilities. There is a Mcdonald’s Drive Through and AEON Jusco is just 2 minutes away from Galleria Equine Park. Grocery shopping are made easy too with many shopping malls such as Giant Hypermarket, The Atmosphere, Pasar Borong Selangor, Putra Walk, IOI Mall Puchong, IOI Resort City Putrajaya and Sierra Zentro only 15 minutes away from Taman Equine. Moreover, 50 metres away from Galleria Equine Park will lead you to SKVE and LDP. Public transportations such as buses and taxis are easily accessible as well. In addition, schools are easily access from Galleria Equine Park as there are a number of international schools and universities in the vicinity of Galleria Equine Park, such as Universiti Putra Malaysia, Multimedia University, Lim Kok Wing University of Creative Technology, John Hopkins University, Segi University, Alice Smith International School, Rafflesia International and Private Schools, ELC International School, Tanarata International School Kajang, Australia International School and Taylor’s International School, Puchong.

Residents will be able to workout at their own leisure time to lose the extra pounds gained and relax at the same time at their own pace and preferred time without worrying about human congestion. Stress? Why not have barbeque sessions with your loved ones at the barbeque area to distress? There are 5 storeys of car pack levels and each unit is given 1 car park bay. In addition, there are 4 lifts per floor so residents do not have to worry about the long waiting time for the lift.
